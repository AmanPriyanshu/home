Bio

I am a First Year Ph.D. student in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech. My advisor is Dr. Munmun De Choudhury and I work in the Social Dynamics and Wellbeing Lab. I did my undergraduate at IIT Kharagpur in Computer Science and Engineering. I have also had industry research experience at Siemens, Infosys Labs and Abzooba.

My primary research area is Social Computing, and I am particularly interested in problems from the domain of Data Science, involving Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, Predictive Analytics and Information Retrieval. I am inclined towards novel research work in these fields, especially taking up practical problems in real-world scenarios and devising flexible and smart solutions which can adapt to changing social dynamics.
